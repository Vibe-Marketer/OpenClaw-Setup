PHYSICAL MAC SETUP
==================

STEP 1: Open Terminal
   - Press Cmd + Space
   - Type: Terminal
   - Press Enter

STEP 2: Copy this ENTIRE line below (all of it, including the quotes):

/bin/bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Vibe-Marketer/OpenClaw-Setup/main/Physical-Mac-Setup/install.sh)"

STEP 3: Paste it into Terminal (Cmd + V) and press Enter

STEP 4: Wait for it to finish, then run: openclaw configure

That's it!
